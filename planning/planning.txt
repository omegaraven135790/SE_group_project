REQUIREMNTS:

User Requirements (UR) 

UR1 The app lets users type in the ingredients they have at home.
UR2 Using those ingredients, the app can suggest healthy recipes the user can make.
UR3 Users can filter the results by dietary preference, such as vegan or vegetarian.
UR4 Users can set a calorie range to narrow down recipe suggestions.
UR5 Users can look for recipes that take under 30 minutes.
UR6 Recipes show nutrition details, including calories, protein, carbs, and fats.
UR7 Users can save recipes to a favourites list.
UR8 Users can share recipes with other people.
UR9 Users can rate recipes.
UR10 The app shows simple health tips together with recipes.
UR11 New users can sign up so their profile, favourites, and credits are saved.
UR12 Returning users can log in securely to access their account.
UR13 If a user forgets their password, they can reset it to regain access.
UR14 Users can log out to keep their account safe on shared devices.
UR15 Users can update profile preferences like diet preference and health goal.
UR16 Free users can see how many credits they still have left.
UR17 For free users, recipe generation is limited by the available credits.
UR18 Users can open an upgrade page to see what premium includes.
UR19 Users can upgrade to premium using the checkout flow shown in the app.
UR20 Premium users can generate a weekly meal plan.
UR21 Admin can view usage metrics to monitor the system.
UR22 Admin can manage user accounts by viewing users and suspending accounts when needed.
UR23 Admin can manage subscription tiers and set credit allocations.
UR24 While a recipe is being generated, the app shows a loading screen.
UR25 When no suitable recipe can be generated, the app shows a clear and friendly message.
UR26 Users can view their saved favourite recipes.
UR27 Premium users can view their weekly meal plan.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)

NFR1 The app should feel easy for first-time users, with clear labels and obvious buttons.
NFR2 Screens should follow a consistent layout and navigation style so users don’t get confused.
NFR3 The design should work well on mobile screens and be comfortable to use with touch.
NFR4 The app should give clear feedback for key states, such as loading, success, errors, and no results.
NFR5 The Figma prototype should be smooth to demo, with working links and no dead ends.
NFR6 Login and upgrade/checkout screens should look professional and trustworthy.
NFR7 Text should be easy to read, and buttons should be large enough to tap comfortably.
