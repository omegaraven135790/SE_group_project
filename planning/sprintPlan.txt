For our project we will have a total of 3 sprints.

how each sprint works:

each sprint last 1 week from starting(unless there is a special reason) with tasks always proceeding through its relevant states(analysis,
design,implementation) and then finally progressing to review where another group member checks the work for bugs or any faults and if all is fine
it is moved to done state. for each state generally 2-3 sentances are written to provide a brief description of what was done. At the end
of each sprint we evaulate how it went and what we learned from it.

1st sprint:

initial analysis and planning done, diagrams such as the use case diagram and class diagram are completed along with the project vision
and justification document.

2nd sprint:

focus on implementation and preparing final report and slides, update burndown chart and begin working on the remaining final deliverables.

3rd sprint:

all tasks should be done or near completion, testing is done, final submission preperations are to be done and assignment submitted and presentation
is done by this point. Burn down chart and other final documents are updated and double checked.
